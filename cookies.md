<h1 class="mb-4">Cookie policy</h1>

<p>This site uses Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors use it, and sets one cookie of its own to remember that you've dismissed the cookie message.</p>

<h2 class="h5 mt-4">Opting out</h2>
<p>You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the <a href="https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on</a>, or by blocking cookies in your browser settings.</p>
