Youth Service User Research

27 February 2025

User research to redesign the referral pathway for a youth mental health support service.

We ran co-design workshops with young people and frontline staff, mapped the existing journey, and tested a simplified referral form with a small pilot cohort.

The redesigned pathway reduced average time-to-first-contact by a third during the pilot.
