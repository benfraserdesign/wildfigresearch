About Wild Fig Research

Wild Fig Research is a non-profit participatory research organisation based in Sheffield, working locally, regionally, nationally and internationally with diverse communities and organisations.

We design and deliver participatory research with communities, VCFSE (voluntary, community, faith and social enterprise) organisations, universities, and the public sector - shifting thinking and practice at every level, from grassroots priorities to institutional decision-making.

We're set up as a company limited by guarantee. This means there are no shareholders, no distributed profit, and all surplus is reinvested in the work.
