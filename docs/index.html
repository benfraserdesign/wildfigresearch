Wild Fig Research is a Sheffield-based organisation that works with communities, VCFSE (voluntary, community, faith and social enterprise) organisations, universities, and the public sector to design and deliver participatory research.

Read more about Wild Fig Research Get in touch

Co-production

Designing research and services together with the people they're for, not just about them.

Participatory research

Methods that put lived experience at the centre, from co-design workshops to peer research.

Qualitative and visual research

Interviews, ethnography and creative methods that surface what numbers alone can't show.

Impact evaluation

Mixed-methods evaluation frameworks that show what's working, for whom, and why.

Theories of change

Clear logic models and outcome measures that connect activity to real change.

Quantitative research

Surveys and statistical analysis that add a robust evidence base alongside qualitative insight.

Research ethics & data management

Rigorous ethical approval, informed consent and secure handling of sensitive data.

Content design & user research

Plain language and accessible content, shaped by research into what users actually need.

Systems change

Working across sectors and services to shift the conditions that hold problems in place.
