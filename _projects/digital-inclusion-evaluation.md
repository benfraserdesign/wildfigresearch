<p class="lead">An evaluation of a two-year digital inclusion programme delivered across a network of community libraries.</p>

<p>Using pre/post surveys, device-loan usage data and interviews with participants and staff, we assessed the programme's impact on digital skills and confidence.</p>

<p>Our findings shaped the funder's decision to extend the programme for a further three years.</p>
