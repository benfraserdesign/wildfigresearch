About Wild Fig Research

Wild Fig Research Ltd is a non-profit participatory research organisation based in Sheffield, set up as a company limited by guarantee - no shareholders, no distributed profit, all surplus reinvested in the work.

We design and deliver participatory research with communities, VCFSE (voluntary, community, faith and social enterprise) organisations, universities, and the public sector - shifting thinking and practice at every level, from grassroots priorities to institutional decision-making.
