<p class="lead">A mixed-methods needs assessment commissioned by a regional health partnership to identify gaps in access to primary care services.</p>

<p>We combined a household survey (n=612), six focus groups and secondary data analysis to produce a set of prioritised recommendations for commissioners.</p>

<p>The final report informed a re-commissioning decision covering three local authority areas.</p>
