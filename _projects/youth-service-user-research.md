<p class="lead">User research to redesign the referral pathway for a youth mental health support service.</p>

<p>We ran co-design workshops with young people and frontline staff, mapped the existing journey, and tested a simplified referral form with a small pilot cohort.</p>

<p>The redesigned pathway reduced average time-to-first-contact by a third during the pilot.</p>
