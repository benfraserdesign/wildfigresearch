<p class="lead mb-2">Wild Fig Research is a non-profit participatory research organisation based in Sheffield, working locally, regionally, nationally and internationally with diverse communities and organisations.</p>
<p class="lead mb-2">We design and deliver participatory research with communities, VCFSE (voluntary, community, faith and social enterprise) organisations, universities, and the public sector - shifting thinking and practice at every level, from grassroots priorities to institutional decision-making.</p>
<p class="lead mb-0">We're set up as a company limited by guarantee. This means there are no shareholders, no distributed profit, and all surplus is reinvested in the work.</p>
